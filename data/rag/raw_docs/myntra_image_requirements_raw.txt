Myntra Fashion Image Guidelines (Unprocessed Raw)

- model shots: mandatory for most fashion categories, STRAIGHT POSE, NATURAL LIGHT. 
- min resolution 1500 * 1500 important for quality.
- Colors must be EXACT, no oversaturation allowed (QC fail risk)
- Dont show accessories if not included.

Photographs should reflect ACTUAL FABRIC DRAPE. 
Heavy editing not allowed.
