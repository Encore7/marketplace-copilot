# Flipkart Image Requirements (Fashion, Apparel, Footwear, Accessories)

This document summarizes Flipkart’s official-style image guidelines for fashion listings.  
These requirements directly influence Listing Quality Score (LQS), search visibility, and return rates.

---

# 1. General Rules for All Flipkart Categories

Flipkart expects product images to be:

- Clear and high resolution  
- Accurate to the product being shipped  
- Free of promotional overlays  
- Consistent in background and lighting  
- Representative of actual color and texture  

Minimum quality standards:

- **Minimum 1000 × 1000 px**
- **White or light-neutral background** preferred  
- **No watermarks**  
- **No text overlays**  
- **No borders**  
- **No collage images** (1 image = 1 view)

---

# 2. Number of Images Required

Flipkart Fashion categories require:

- **Minimum 3 images**
- **Recommended 5–7 images**

Required views:

1. Front view  
2. Side view  
3. Back view  
4. Close-up of material / pattern  
5. Packaging or accessories (optional)

Footwear requires:
- Top view  
- Side view  
- Sole view  
- Close-up of material  

Wallets & bags:
- Front, back, top  
- Inside compartments  
- Close-up of stitching/material  

---

# 3. Background Requirements

Allowed:

- White (#FFFFFF)  
- Light neutral backgrounds (light grey, beige)

Not allowed:

- Bright colored backgrounds  
- Distracting patterns or textures  
- Outdoor backgrounds unless in lifestyle images  
- Model backgrounds that hide real color of product

Lifestyle images (with model) are allowed if:
- they show accurate fit, fall, and drape  
- they do not include promotional or distracting props  

---

# 4. Image Content Rules (Strict Violations)

Flipkart suppresses listings containing:

## 4.1 Promotional Content
- “Best Price”, “Offer”, “Sale”
- Percentages (e.g., “50% OFF”)
- Free shipping icons  
- EMI text  
- Star ratings  
- Stamp-like icons (“Premium Quality”)

## 4.2 Competitor or Brand Misuse
- Logos of other brands  
- Images showing branded packaging not belonging to seller

## 4.3 Misleading Visuals
- Over-edited colors  
- Extreme brightening or saturation  
- Using different product than actual SKU  
- Stock / catalog images from internet  
- Fake materials (leather look overlays)

## 4.4 Human Models – Rules
Allowed:
- Correct depiction of fit  
- Natural lighting  
- Clean background  

Not allowed:
- Face visibility (for some apparel categories)  
- Inaccurate representation of color or material  
- Suggestive poses  

---

# 5. Category-Specific Requirements

## 5.1 Apparel (Shirts, Kurtas, Tops)
- Front, side, back mandatory  
- Model shots allowed  
- Close-up on fabric texture  
- Accurate color representation is critical  
- Size chart image optional but recommended  

## 5.2 Sarees / Ethnic Wear
- Full-drape image required  
- Pallu and border close-up  
- Blouse piece indication (if included)  
- Avoid excessive jewelry that distracts from the product  

## 5.3 Footwear
- Side, top, back, sole views  
- Close-up of mesh/knit/woven details  
- True color representation  
- Indoor consistent lighting  

## 5.4 Wallets, Bags, Belts
- Show compartments clearly  
- Show interior lining and stitching  
- Show scale/size with an object (optional but helpful)  

---

# 6. Technical Specifications

- 1:1 aspect ratio recommended for main image  
- No heavy compression; maintain clarity  
- PNG or JPEG accepted  
- Max file size recommended: 2–3 MB  

---

# 7. How the Copilot Should Use This

When optimizing Flipkart listings:

1. Check if minimum number of views is present (3–5+).  
2. Validate that images do not contain promotional text or overlays.  
3. Recommend additional angles (sole view, fabric close-up).  
4. Ensure colors appear consistent with description.  
5. Flag potential violations:
   - stock images  
   - misleading enhancements  
   - wrong materials  

6. Suggest improvements aligned with LQS and con
