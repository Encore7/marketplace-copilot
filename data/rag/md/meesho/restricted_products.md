# Meesho Restricted & Prohibited Products  
Applicable to Fashion, Footwear, Accessories, Beauty, Home & Lifestyle Sellers

Meesho enforces strict rules to maintain customer trust and safety.  
Listings violating these rules may be removed, penalized, or lead to account suspension.

This document outlines categories, keywords, materials, and claims that are prohibited.

---

# 1. Completely Prohibited Categories

These are NOT allowed on Meesho under any circumstances:

- Medicines, supplements, weight-loss pills
- Sexual wellness products
- Tobacco, gutka, cigarettes, vapes
- Alcohol, spirits, beer, energy shots
- Weapons, knives, sharp tools with blades
- Firecrackers, explosives
- Live animals
- Hazardous chemicals
- Counterfeit branded products
- Adult content
- Used or refurbished items

---

# 2. Restricted / High-Risk Categories (Fashion & Beauty Related)

These can be listed **only if they meet specific rules**:

## 2.1 Cosmetics & Skincare
Not allowed:
- fairness/whitening claims
- anti-aging “guarantees”
- miracle/overnight results
- medical-sounding terms (“clinically proven”)

Allowed only if:
- label ingredients match photos
- expiry date provided
- no medicinal claims

## 2.2 Body Shapers, Fitness Items
Not allowed:
- “instant inch loss”
- “fat burning”
- “weight reduction guarantee”

## 2.3 Footwear & Apparel Materials
Forbidden:
- real leather claims without proof  
- silk/satin if images don’t match  
- gold-plated/jewelry claims without hallmarking  

## 2.4 Kids’ Apparel & Footwear
Not allowed:
- misleading cotton claims (must match images)
- choking hazard items (small detachable parts)
- sharp accessories (metal tips/pins)

---

# 3. Prohibited Keywords (Common in Fashion Listings)

Avoid these keywords in titles/descriptions:

- “Guaranteed”
- “Authentic Nike/Adidas/etc.”
- “Original branded”
- “Fat burner”
- “Weight loss”
- “Instant results”
- “Magic cure”
- “Doctor recommended”
- “High returns” / “viral product”

---

# 4. Prohibited Claims in Listings

## 4.1 Fabric & Material Claims
Not allowed:
- “100% pure cotton” when actual is cotton blend
- misleading PU leather as real leather
- synthetic silk labeled as “pure silk”

## 4.2 Performance Claims
Not allowed:
- “Sweat-proof guaranteed”
- “Durability guaranteed”
- “Shock-absorbent proven”
- “Running shoe improves speed”

## 4.3 Medical Claims
Strictly forbidden:
- relieves pain  
- cures joint discomfort  
- orthopedic benefits  
- posture correction guarantees  

---

# 5. Image-Based Restrictions

Forbidden:

- watermarks  
- promotional overlays (“50% off”)  
- branded logos not matching product  
- stock images taken from internet  
- showing more items than included  
- misleading color editing  
- product bundles not actually delivered  

---

# 6. Packaging & Disclosure Rules

Sellers MUST NOT:

- show premium packaging that isn’t delivered  
- misrepresent quantity  
- misrepresent size/fit  
- hide defects in images  
- provide misleading size charts  

---

# 7. What This Means for the Copilot

The copilot should:

1. Scan titles, bullets, attributes for restricted keywords  
2. Detect fake material claims (common issue on Meesho)  
3. Flag medical or performance-related claims  
4. Check for misleading packaging or imagery  
5. Highlight inconsistent fabric/size details  
6. Provide corrections aligned with Meesho rules  

This enables automated compliance analysis for Meesho listings.
