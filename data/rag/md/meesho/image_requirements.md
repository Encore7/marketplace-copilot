# Meesho Image Requirements (Fashion, Apparel, Footwear, Accessories)

Meesho’s audience relies heavily on images to judge material quality and fit.  
Accurate, clear, and non-promotional images are mandatory.

---

# 1. General Image Guidelines

- Minimum resolution: **900×900 px**  
- White or neutral background preferred  
- No watermarks  
- No text overlays  
- No logos  
- No price or discount graphics  
- No stock/internet images  

Images must represent the **actual product** sent to the buyer.

---

# 2. Required Number of Images

Recommended:

- **At least 3 images**  
- Preferably 4–6 images

Required views:

### Apparel
- Front  
- Back  
- Close-up of fabric  
- Model shots allowed but optional  

### Footwear
- Side  
- Top  
- Back  
- Sole  
- Material close-up  

### Bags & Wallets
- Front  
- Back  
- Inside compartments  
- Close-up of stitching/material  

---

# 3. Background Rules

Allowed:
- White background  
- Light grey or beige  
- Soft gradients if subtle  

Not allowed:
- Bright colors  
- Busy patterns  
- Outdoor background for main image  
- Distracting props  

Lifestyle images are allowed as secondary images, not the main one.

---

# 4. Image Content Restrictions

Forbidden:

- logos of popular brands  
- stock images from Google  
- face shots with heavy makeup/filters  
- unrealistic colors due to over-editing  
- misleading textures  
- showing more items than included  
- accessories not provided with product  

Meesho moderates images manually when flagged.

---

# 5. Model Image Guidelines

Allowed:
- natural poses  
- accurate garment drape  
- no heavy color editing  
- no hidden product parts  

Not allowed:
- revealing or suggestive poses  
- extreme fashion photography editing  
- using celebrity-like images from internet  

Model photos MUST reflect actual product color and pattern.

---

# 6. Technical Requirements

- JPEG or PNG  
- Square aspect ratio recommended  
- File size < 2MB for faster load  

---

# 7. How the Copilot Should Use This

The copilot should:

1. Check if minimum required views are available  
2. Flag:
   - promotional overlays  
   - fake materials  
   - stock images  
   - inaccurate colors  
3. Suggest additional shots (fabric close-up, sole view, compartments)  
4. Ensure main image follows neutral background rules  
5. Provide actionable improvements for Meesho image compliance
