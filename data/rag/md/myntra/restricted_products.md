# Myntra Restricted & Prohibited Products  
Applicable to Apparel, Footwear, Accessories, Beauty

Myntra enforces category-specific restrictions because of its premium fashion positioning.

---

# 1. Completely Prohibited Categories

- Counterfeit branded apparel/footwear  
- Unbranded or generic fashion products  
- Products with misleading brand claims  
- Adult content  
- Used/refurbished items  
- Health/medical products  
- Weight loss or slimming products  
- Hazardous items  
- Real animal fur products  
- Offensive prints or imagery  

---

# 2. Material-Related Restrictions

## Forbidden:
- Fake “100% cotton” claims  
- PU leather labeled as “genuine leather”  
- Synthetic silk labeled as “pure silk”  
- Real fur (fox/mink)  
- Unethical leather sources  

## Allowed only if verified:
- Genuine leather with certification  
- Handwoven fabrics  
- Organic cotton  

---

# 3. Footwear Restrictions

Forbidden:
- fake “running technology”  
- misleading cushioning claims  
- orthopedic/medical statements  

Allowed if truthful:
- mesh upper  
- EVA foam sole  
- rubber outsole  

---

# 4. Apparel Restrictions

Not allowed:
- exaggerated “slimming” claims  
- body shaping guarantees  
- incorrect size charts  
- prints/colors drastically different from images  

---

# 5. Beauty & Accessory Restrictions (Relevant for Fashion Sellers)

Prohibited:
- fairness claims  
- medical claims  
- chemical ingredients not disclosed  
- fake “dermatologically tested”  

---

# 6. Keyword Restrictions

Disallowed:
- “Guaranteed”  
- “Original Nike/Adidas” etc.  
- “Magic slimming”  
- “Medical grade”  
- “Instant whitening”  
- “Premium original leather” (without proof)  

---

# 7. Image-Based Restrictions

Not allowed:
- misleading edited colors  
- stock photography  
- provocative model poses  
- showing unmatched accessories  
- using branded backdrops  

---

# 8. How The Copilot Should Use This File

- Detect fake material or brand claims  
- Flag prohibited keywords  
- Identify misleading fit/size/material statements  
- Highlight inconsistent photos  
- Ensure alignment between claims and product visuals
